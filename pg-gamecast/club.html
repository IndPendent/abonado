The best way to enjoy our wines.

La Cava Chahue has always had two clear visions: provide a service that adapts to our clients’ convenience and bring the finest wines we can find closer to them. For this reason, we invite you to become one of our Amigos de La Cava.

The members of this new and exclusive community will receive on a monthly basis, in the comfort of their own home, wines cases with extraordinary bottles, specially selected by our experts, as well as additional benefits.

¿How does Amigos de la Cava work?


You have two options to choose from: Amigos de La Cava and Amigos de La Cava Plus. You can read about our two types of memberships here.
